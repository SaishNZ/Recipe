Recipe- Coconut Barfi

1) Take 1 bowl of wet grated coconut + 1 bowl of milk + 1 bowl of sugar.

2) Grease a pan/kadhai with ghee.

3) Put all the three ingredients in a pan/kadhai.

4) Boil the mixture till it gets thick.

5) If the mixture is not sticking to the pan/kadhai, then it is ready to be poured in a flat plate greased with ghee.

6) As soon as you transfer the mixture in the flat plate, flatten it quickly with a spatula.

7) Cut it into pieces of desired shapes before it cools.

8) Let it set for 30 minutes.

And now its ready to eat!
